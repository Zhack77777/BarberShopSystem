About Jay-R Barbershop

Cultivating community through precision grooming since 2021

Founded by Catalino "Jay-R" Escudero Jr. and Maria Lina Escudero, our Lower Bicutan studio blends timeless barber craft with modern hospitality. Every cut, shave, and conversation reflects our love for the neighborhood.

With 23+ years of experience, Jay-R leads a team of master barbers who obsess over details—fades, blade work, skin prep, and product knowledge—so you leave refreshed and confident.
