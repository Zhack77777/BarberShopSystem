About Us

An elevated grooming experience rooted in community

Since March 2021, Jay-R Barbershop has shaped the styles of Taguig City with precision cuts, hot towel shaves, and a relaxed lounge vibe. Every visit feels like hanging out with friends who just so happen to be master barbers.

We obsess over details—blade work, fades, skin prep, and the conversation—so you can walk out refreshed and confident.
