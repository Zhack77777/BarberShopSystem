Our Story

Founded in 2020, Jay-R Barbershop was born from a passion for the timeless art of barbering. What started as a small neighborhood shop has grown into a beloved establishment known for its exceptional service and welcoming atmosphere.

Our founder, Jay-R, has over 15 years of experience in the industry and has trained under some of the best barbers in the country. His vision was to create a space where clients could receive top-notch grooming services while feeling like part of the family.
