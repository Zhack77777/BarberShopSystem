Our Story

Founded in 2021, Jay-R Barbershop was born from a passion for the timeless art of barbering. What began as a small neighborhood shop has grown into a beloved establishment known for exceptional service and a friendly, community-focused environment.

Owned by husband and wife Catalino "Jay-R" Escudero Jr. and Maria Lina Escudero, the barbershop takes its name from Jay-R’s nickname. With over 23 years of experience in the industry, Jay-R has dedicated his life to mastering the craft of barbering. On March 28, 2021, he and Maria Lina opened their own barbershop in Lower Bicutan, Taguig City, turning their vision into reality. Maria Lina assists in the shop, helping ensure smooth operations and a welcoming experience for every client.

At Jay-R Barbershop, every haircut is more than just a service – it is an experience. The team of skilled barbers provides personalized attention to each client, ensuring everyone leaves looking and feeling their best.
